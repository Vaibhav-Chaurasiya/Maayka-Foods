About Maayka Foods

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Maayka Foods began as a small-scale food processing unit with a vision to deliver high-quality, affordable snacks to the Indian market. Over the years, we've grown into a reputable FMCG manufacturer serving retailers and distributors across North India.

Our name 'Maayka' symbolizes our commitment to providing food products that meet the highest standards of quality and taste, just like homemade food from one's maternal home.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality, safe, and delicious food products that cater to the evolving tastes of Indian consumers while maintaining affordability and accessibility.

Manufacturing Capabilities

Our 20,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Ghaziabad is equipped with modern machinery and follows strict hygiene protocols. We have the capacity to produce over 50 tons of food products monthly across various categories.
